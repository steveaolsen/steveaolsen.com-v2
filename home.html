Birthday/Age Calculator

The Birthday/Age Calculator took more work than might meet the eye. For example, one might think that by simply subtracting dates from a current date we get a working program. Unfortunatey, if that was the case, a person who was born on January 20, 2000, running the program on January 19, 2018, would be shown as 18 years, 0 months, and -1 days old. To correct this issue, I used a series of if/else statements which would determine the current month and day and compare it to the birth-day and birth-month. The code would then exchange the equivolent amount of time (-1 year for +12 months) and figure the correct amount for output. I even coded it to determine the current month and created if/else statements to trade the correct number of days for months containing 31, 30 and 28 days. It does figure for leap years, since a leap year is still one year, but it wont figure the extra day in February for a current leap year. You can hire me to make that for you though!

The program determines if your day or month is plural or singular and displays "days/months" or "day/month" respectively. It doesn't do that for years because I have determined that very few one year olds will be using my program. If you are one and already using JavaScript to determine your age, you are beyond what I can teach you. Also if you enter "0" for you Year, Month and Day, the program will recognize that you are Jesus and greet you accordingly.
